About me

Since July 2017 I'm a PhD student of Stefan Mangard in the Secure Systems (SeSys) group at the Institute of Applied Information Processing and Communications (IAIK) in Graz, Austria. My current research focus is implementations security/attacks, symmetric, and post-quantum cryptography. I received my MSc in Computer Science at Graz University of Technology with focus on IT Security. My Master's Thesis is about Single-Trace Attacks on Lattice-based Encryption and was supervised by Stefan Mangard and Peter Pessl.
